Recipe Haven

This name was selected because it conveys a welcoming space for cooking enthusiasts to discover, share, and enjoy a variety of recipes. The term "Haven" suggests a safe and comforting place, aligning with the idea of home-cooked meals.

Optional domain availability: recipehaven.com (check for availability)

Site Purpose

Recipe Haven serves as a comprehensive platform for food lovers to explore a diverse collection of recipes. The site will allow users to browse, filter, and add their own recipes, providing a community-driven experience. It will also feature user preferences for light or dark mode, enhancing usability.

Scenarios

1. What types of recipes can I find on Recipe Haven?

This question addresses users looking for specific cuisines or difficulty levels in cooking.

2. How can I submit my own recipe to the site?

This scenario helps guide users interested in contributing their culinary creations to the collection.

Color Schema

Usage:

  • Background (#34403a): --color-background for the overall site background.
  • Primary Text (#ffffff): --color-text-primary for main content and body text.
  • Secondary Text (#285238): --color-text-secondary for secondary information or less prominent text.
  • Accents (#04e824): --color-accent for buttons and interactive elements.
  • Highlights (#18ff6d): --color-highlight for important highlights or notifications.
  • Headings (#1dd755): --color-heading for headings and subheadings to create a cohesive look.
