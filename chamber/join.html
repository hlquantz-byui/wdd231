NP Membership Benefits

As a Non-Profit Membership, you receive:

  • Access to community resources and support.
  • Participation in special non-profit events.
  • Networking opportunities with other non-profits.
  • Free advertising on our website's non-profit section.

Bronze Membership Benefits

As a Bronze Member, you enjoy:

  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • 10% discount on events and workshops.
  • Access to exclusive training sessions.
  • Spotlight feature on our homepage for one month.

Silver Membership Benefits

As a Silver Member, you receive:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • 15% discount on events and workshops.
  • Priority access to special events.
  • Featured article in our monthly newsletter.

Gold Membership Benefits

As a Gold Member, you enjoy:

  • All Silver Membership benefits.
  • 20% discount on events and workshops.
  • Exclusive invitations to VIP events.
  • Personalized advertising options on our website.
  • One-on-one consultation sessions with our experts.
